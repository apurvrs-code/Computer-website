Computer Chapter 1 - Internet Basics

What is Internet History of Internet Web Browser & Search Engine Email Cloud Computing Internet Safety

The Internet is a global network of computers connected to share information and resources.

It allows communication through email, websites, social media, and more.

The Internet started in the 1960s as ARPANET by the US Department of Defense.

It grew over decades to become a worldwide communication network connecting billions of devices.

A web browser is software used to access the Internet, like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.

A search engine, like Google or Bing, helps you find information on the Internet.

Email (Electronic Mail) is a method of sending digital messages over the Internet.

Popular services include Gmail, Yahoo Mail, and Outlook.

Cloud computing allows storing and accessing data over the Internet instead of local devices.

Examples: Google Drive, Dropbox, Microsoft OneDrive.

Always protect personal information, use strong passwords, and avoid suspicious links.

Secure connections (HTTPS) and antivirus software help stay safe online.
